Higher Learning: Maximizing Your College Experience
is the essential tool for students planning on, transitioning to, or currently enrolled in college.

Packed with inspirational insights and game-changing techniques, this completely rewritten third edition of Higher Learning pushes students to become their fullest selves on campus. Topics include time management, leadership, relationship-building, financial planning and paying for school, career prep, and a host of academic strategies for students to reach their college goals.

About Brian Peterson

Brian Peterson is the Director of Makuu, the University of Pennsylvania's Black Cultural Center, and a lecturer at Penn. Over his twenty years in higher education he has built and facilitated college access and completion programs across all levels including Ase Academy (junior high), Summer Impact (high school), #GoHigher202 (summer bridge), and the Paul Robeson and Anna Julia Scholars Program (undergraduate).
