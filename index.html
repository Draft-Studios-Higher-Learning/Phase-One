About Brian Peterson

Brian Peterson is the Director of Makuu, the University of Pennsylvania's Black Cultural Center, and a lecturer at Penn. Over his twenty years in higher education he has built and facilitated college access and completion programs across all levels including Ase Academy (junior high), Summer Impact (high school), #GoHigher202 (summer bridge), and the Paul Robeson and Anna Julia Scholars Program (undergraduate).
